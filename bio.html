BIO

ABOUT

Everton Ramos de Oliveira born in Brazil in December 1994, a passionate about electronic music since adolescence, who lives in the city of Barueri, started attending electronic music parties since 2010 and was enjoying this scene more and more. Until one day he was curious to know where all these sound effects come from and how the DJs of the time did to play, when one day he met a colleague who explained how it worked and with a lot of curiosity began to search the internet starting to take an interest in the style of Electro House, Progressive House and Big Room from the EDM scene.
Over time it was improving following several tutorials on the internet, but began to steady in musical productions doing the online course of production and mixing at Pointblanc music school, in addition to taking DJ lessons with record playing in a community center in your city.
In addition to his dedication chose the name Deens Ramos, in view of a stage name that matches the appearance in the musical genre, began gradually creating, Some simple tracks without much style, until his first track to start exciting was the track love sensation, a track that was made with singer Loleatta holloway's vocals, at the time very excited about the big room scene was looking more and more his style and career participating in the remix contest on WAVO with the remix of track KLP - Melt. In addition to the production of EDM was also curious to create a more tropical style between moombahton, Funk Carioca and trap releasing a remix of track Major Lazer - Sua Cara (feat. Anitta Vittar Pabllo), which came to be recognized by artist DANNIC in the FONK RADIO program episode 97.
In addition to remixes with style very typical of Brazil featured in electronic music, other tracks remixed as Mc Fioti Future J Balvin Stefflon Don Juan Magan - Bum Bum Tam Tam , Clean Bandit - Mama and Borgeous & Zack Martino - Make Me Yours on SKIO music remix contest, the music that caught a lot of attention was also the track Vai Dançar made with the vignette of the Jack O Matador of a former country double called léo canhoto & Robertinho from 1986.
With each passing day Deens Ramos becomes an increasingly undefeated and creative artist, seeking a vibrant audience that likes emotion and a lot of dancing sound.
